    Quick answers

    Is everything really halal? +
    Yes — 100% halal. Every meat we serve, from the Angus smash patties to the crispy chicken, is fully halal. It's the foundation of the house.
    Do you deliver? Is there a minimum? +
    We deliver across Sheffield "S" postcodes — there's a £20 minimum for delivery. Nearby zones (S1, S2, S3, S6) usually take 20–30 min, the wider area 30–45 min. You can also order via Just Eat or Uber Eats, or collect in store with no minimum.
    I have an allergy — can you help? +
    Allergen info is available on request. Give your branch a quick call before ordering and the team will walk you through what's safe — everything is made fresh, so we can often adapt a dish.
    How late are you open? +
    Open till midnight Sunday–Thursday, and till 12:30am on Friday & Saturday (Friday we open at 3pm, every other day at noon). Both branches keep the same hours.
    Where do I collect my order? +
    Whichever branch suits you: 158 Infirmary Rd (S6 3DH) or 156 St Mary's Rd (S2 4AX) — the one on St Mary's has a sisters' seating area. Collection orders are usually ready in 15–20 minutes.
    Can you handle big or office orders? +
    Absolutely — we regularly feed whole offices, boxed and labelled. Call ahead with your order and pickup/delivery time and we'll have everything hot and ready together.

    Something about an order you've placed? Call your branch — it's the fastest way to reach the kitchen while your food's being made.
